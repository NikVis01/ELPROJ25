Swimming Conditions Based on Turbidity

Turbidity is a measure of water clarity. The following guidelines can help determine if swimming conditions are acceptable:

  • 80-100%: Excellent clarity. Ideal for swimming.
  • 60-79%: Good clarity. Swimming is generally safe.
  • 40-59%: Moderate clarity. Caution is advised, especially for children or those with sensitive skin.
  • 20-39%: Poor clarity. Swimming is not recommended.
  • 0-19%: Very poor clarity. Avoid swimming due to potential health risks.
